What is TressJS?

Tress is a JavaScript based engine for playing Tress, an abstract strategy board game I created, inspired by Chess. I decided to make Tress into a browser game to test the game more easily, to make the game accessible to friends and family, and to improve my skills as a programmer and game designer.

The object of Tress is to move your King and Queen (or Crown and Coronet) into the red zone in the center of the board, known as the Fortress, and to prevent your opponent from doing the same. At your disposal you have six Knights (or Swords), two Guardians (or Shields), and two Pikemen (or Spears), which you must use to control the board and safely escort your King and Queen to the Fortress. You should also be aware that if your Pikemen or Guardians can enter the Fortress uncontested by the enemy, they can promote to more powerful pieces! Pikemen can become Lancers, and Guardians can become the fearsome Sentinel, which can quickly dominate the board.
